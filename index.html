03 / Diamond Pricing Analysis

What Actually
Drives Diamond Value?

Engaged as an analytics consultant for BrightStone Jewelers to diagnose mispricing across 59,000 diamonds and build a predictive model to support a more accurate, data-driven pricing strategy.

Key Contributions

Scoped and framed a consulting engagement around a real business problem, identifying which diamond attributes drive price and where mispricing occurs
Built and tuned a random forest model on 59,000 records, achieving 97.4% accuracy and reducing RMSE by 84% over the naive baseline
Applied Shapley value analysis to interpret individual predictions, translating black-box model outputs into explainable, attribute-level pricing contributions
Investigated interaction effects between carat, clarity, and color to surface nuanced pricing dynamics beyond simple variable rankings
Designed and delivered a full client-facing presentation to a consulting professor with actionable recommendations for procurement, marketing, and dynamic pricing strategy
